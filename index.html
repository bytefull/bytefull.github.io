How it works
From tap to open — in under a second

Tapify replaces your key fobs with a phone. The authentication is cryptographic and fully local — no internet required.

📱

Member downloads app

Creates an account. A private key is generated and stored securely in the phone's hardware keystore — it never leaves the device.

›
✋

One-tap enrolment

Admin puts the device in enrolment mode. Member taps their phone. Done in under 30 seconds.

›
🔐

Cryptographic auth

Every tap triggers a live challenge-response. The credential cannot be copied, shared, or replayed.

›
🚪

Door opens

Relay triggers. Access granted. Admin sees it in the log. All offline, all local.

Why Tapify
Built differently, on purpose

Most access control systems were designed for a world of key cards and cloud servers. Tapify wasn't.

📡

Offline-first

Access decisions happen on the device. No internet, no cloud subscription, no single point of failure. The door opens even if your WiFi goes down.

🔒

Uncloneable credentials

Standard NFC fobs can be duplicated in seconds with cheap hardware. Tapify uses cryptographic challenge-response — every tap is unique and irreproducible.

🔌

Works with existing infrastructure

Relay output connects to any electric gate, turnstile, or door lock. No rewiring, no ripping out existing hardware.

🖥️

Local browser dashboard

Admins manage users, memberships, and access logs from any browser on the local network. No app install, no cloud login required.

📊

Full access audit log

See who entered, when, and how often. Useful for disputes, security reviews, and understanding how your space is actually used.

☁️

Optional cloud sync

Want remote management or multi-site visibility? Cloud sync is available as an opt-in — never a requirement.
